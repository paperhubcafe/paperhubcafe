ABOUT THE CAFE

The humble beginning of this cafe, like any other humble beginning, came from the collective ideas of a group of college students crying over their blank research papers. They were not able to find a venue to meet that is both comfortable and convenient. They were tired from running back and forth from the cafeteria and library of their school grounds whilst researching for their school papers. A few years later the group decided to get together and make their idea a reality. They have managed to combine the comfort airy feel of a small cafe, with the accessibility of a fully furnished Library complete with an internet corner. Collecting research-friendly references, various literary text books as well as light heart and best selling modern day novels to fill the shelves and tables.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Liam Brown

Paperhub Cafe prides itself with great service and friendly atmosphere. A place where students, writers, artists and book enthusiasts get together under a calmy, and homely air. The aroma of freshly brewed tea and coffee, quiet flips of the textured fwips of book pages, and the occasional hums of the running oven. We offer services that would help students with their research, projects, and assignments. Fully equiped computer and internet area and various reference materials at our library. Customers are allowed borrow and use the books offered.
